Chicken Parmesan

Description

There is no better feeling than eating a home cooked meal. So why not make it the best meal possible and cook yourself some chicken parmesan?

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 4 oz mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 Tbsp of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Trim and then slice chicken breasts length-wise. You should have 4 total cutlets.
  2. Stir flour, salt, pepper in bowl. In separate bowl, whisk 2 eggs until frothy. In third bowl, stir the bread crumbs with parmesan cheese.
  3. Dip both sides of cutlets into flour and then into beaten eggs. Finally, dip into breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat large non-stick pan over medium heat. Coat pan with olive oil. Sautee each cutlet 3-4 mins per side.
  5. Pour 1.5 cups of marinara into a casserole baking dish. Lay chicken over sauce and top with remaining 1.5 cups of marinara. Cover with shredded mozzarella and bake at 425 F for 15 minutes.
