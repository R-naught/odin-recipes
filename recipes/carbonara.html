Carbonara

Description

Have you ever wanted the taste of Italy in your own home without going to the hole-in-the-wall pizza shop? Then you've come to the right place! This carbonara recipe will leave you fully satisfied. Just don't mention anything about a British version of this dish...

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Linguine
  • 4 Eggs
  • Parmigiano reggiano
  • 6 oz of Guanciale
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • Parsley (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cut guanciale in 0.5 cm slices. Then cut into batons.
  2. Whisk eggs and yolks in a large bowl until combined. Add parmesan and pepper and stir.
  3. Bring 4 quarts of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until desired texture.
  4. Save 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest from the pasta.
  5. Cook guanciale in a nonstick pan over medium heat. Cook until golden (4-5 minutes). Add minced garlic in the last minute.
  6. Place cooked pasta into pan with guanciale and toss.
  7. Transfer pasta and guanciale into the bowl with egg mixture. Add 0.5 cup of pasta water and stir for 1 minute.
  8. Transfer pasta into warm bowls and serve immediately with parsley.
