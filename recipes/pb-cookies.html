Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Cookies. Peanut Butter. Need I say more? On the best of days, these might even be better chocolate chip cookies. Maybe...

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Mix maple syrup and peanut butter in a deep bowl until combined.
  2. Cover bowl with cling film and put in freezer for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 355 F and line baking tray with parchment paper.
  4. Scoop 12 cookies from bowl and transfer to baking tray with 2 inches between each scoop.
  5. Gently press fork to make cross hatch pattern on top of cookies.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Be careful not to overbake.
